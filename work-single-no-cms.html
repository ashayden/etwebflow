Invisible Office

Now You See It, Now You Don’t

Hocus Pocus Ltd., a leading tech company specializing in augmented reality (AR), wanted to showcase their cutting-edge technology in a way that was both innovative and memorable. They approached The Smiling Agency with the challenge of creating a unique campaign to demonstrate their AR capabilities to potential clients and investors.

‍

‍

Challenge

‍

We decided to highlight Hocus Pocus Ltd.’s AR technology by creating an unforgettable, interactive experience: The Invisible Office. The concept was simple yet captivating—using AR, we made their entire office disappear, giving employees the ability to toggle between their regular office environment and a serene beach scene at the tap of a button.

Strategy

‍

The Smiling Agency turned our vision into a reality—quite literally! The Invisible Office was a stroke of genius that perfectly showcased our AR technology in a fun and engaging way. The feedback has been overwhelmingly positive, and we’ve seen a substantial boost in interest and business opportunities. We couldn’t have asked for a better partner to bring this project to life.
