Welcome to The Smiling Agency, where creativity meets comedy! We believe that laughter is the best way to connect, create, and inspire. Our mission is to infuse every project with humor and innovation, making the world a happier place, one smile at a time.
Founded in a small
Amsterdam café
Founders - Jessica and Jan
What began as a few light-hearted campaigns quickly blossomed into a full-fledged agency known for its quirky, humorous approach to branding and marketing. Today, we’re proud to bring a smile to our clients’ faces and a laugh to their audiences.
